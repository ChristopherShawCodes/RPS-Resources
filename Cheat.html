Programs


- Nex
- CCC
- Parts Fixer- converts all parts to aftermarket for bidding EVERYTHING sent out must first go through parts fixer before exporting to ops trax and parts trader.
- OPS— compare pricing on every job between parts trader and ops trax always chose best cost for shop, unless it violates insurance guidelines (all listed in training site)
- Parts Trader— run every order through parts trader as we are graded by many drps on parts trader quotes.

NO’S


- Used radiators or condensors
- Used quarter panels
- Promo pricing
- Fitparts or Kapa auto parts - they do not warranty parts or accept returns
- Ordering from CCC
- Adhesive templates
- Meyer for CSAA
- Go parts— YOU CAN USE GO PARTS FOR PROGRESSIVE WE ARE WORKING ON HAVING IT REMOVED BUT AS OF RIGHT NOW USE GO PARTS FOR PROGRESSIVE ONLY!

Per SOP


- When a file comes to RPS it should be “ready to send to insurance company” meaning that, advisor reports should be ran, all “reasonable 11’s” should be cleared or documented.
Reman bumpers (Bay Area & First Quality bumpers) and used parts from weirdo vendors that do not bid in parts trader should be verified.
Estimate should be exported to Nex and work order should be opened.
- OPS does not export back to ccc, please check prices in parts trader and check for better deals, but any price or part changes (ie. oem price matches via ops) for parts ordered via ops need to be manually changed in CCC, and most times marked as ordered in nex in purchase assistant, unless your store has been linked to ops most have not.

Dealers that must be ordered via ops trax are:
- Honda for Future Honda when applicable (sometimes Future will bid in parts trader but if they do not then order honda out of ops trax via future) 32% if ordered ops over autonation at 30%
- Nissan- Nissan Rebate program anything under 50% in parts trader needs to be ordered via ops
- Chevy
- BMW
- Jimmy Vassar Toyota when applicable will give you 36% over preferred vendor Maita at 35% Parts trader- does export back to ccc for parts price changes which need to be addressed, and stores are linked to parts trader, but always check to make sure that they are marked ordered in Nex via purchase assistant.
